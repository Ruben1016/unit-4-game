The Bros Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four bros below. By clicking on a bro you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the number.

The value of each bro is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each bro.
